Chat Bench is a local-first AI chat workspace. Conversations stay on your machine. There are no accounts to create, no sync to a server you don't own, and no retention policy to read, because there is nothing on the other end holding your history.

Point it at any model you like, local or remote, and switch between them as the task changes. It is built for real work rather than just chat: generate and edit images, documents, presentations, and spreadsheets in the same place you are having the conversation.

Part of the Benchlabs suite of open source developer tools, which share the same local-first, privacy-first approach.
