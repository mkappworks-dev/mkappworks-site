Design Bench is a design and prototyping tool built for developers and builders rather than full-time designers. Inspect live components directly, reading their spacing, typography, and color without opening Figma or digging through a separate design file.

From there you can annotate what you are looking at and export assets straight out, keeping the design side of a project inside the same workflow as the code.

Part of the Benchlabs suite of open source developer tools.
